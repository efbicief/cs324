How to play

Use the left mouse button to move around the map, the right mouse button to rotate the map,a nd the scroll wheel to zoom in and out.

Your objective is to deliver people to their destination stations. Over time, people will appeat at stations - you can see how many people are waiting at a station by selecting "Create Journey" and hovering over a platform.

To place train tracks, select "Place rails" and draw rails between stations. Each station has 2 platforms. Be careful, tracks are permenant! When placing, you cannot place tracks that are too short or long, or at an extreme angle.

To send trains between stations, you must create a journey by selecting "Create Journey", and selecting the platforms you wish your train to go to in order.

To delete a journey, or change the frequency of trains on a journey, use the "Manage Journeys" menu.

You may pause the game by clicking "Pause", or opening any other menu.

The game ends once the time reaches 500. You will lose 50 points for each train journey, and gain 1 point for each person delivered to their destination, so make sure to plan your journeys well. Good luck!
